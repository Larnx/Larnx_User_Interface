Personal Background

Kestutis was born in Akron Ohio on September 28th, 1995. Kestutis is the son of Darius Subacius and Anna Miloslavskaia. Darius moved to the United States from Lithuania after completing his bachelors degree in Engineering at the Moscow Institute of Technology. Anna is from Moscow, and earned her bachelors in Radiology. The two of t hem moved to the US in 1994, and have since lived in Ohio, Colorado, Texas, and Massachusetts where they reside today. Today, Darius works in Viasat as a communications engineer, and Anna works in Newton Welsley Hospital as a radiologist technician.


Kestutis attended Groton-Dunstable Regional High School until graduating in the Spring of 2014. But the fall of 2014, Kestutis was attending the Boston University College of Engineering, studying Biomedical and Computer Engineering. Prior to attending Boston University, Kestutis was a cross-country and track-and-field athlete in his high school.
